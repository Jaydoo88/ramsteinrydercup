🏌️‍♂️ Rules & Expectations
Setting the tone for our inaugural trip — have a great time, respect the space, and don’t be the problem.

🔑 Core Principles
Respect the House
We’re staying in a premium property — treat it better than your own. Clean up after yourself and keep shared spaces presentable.

Respect the Neighborhood
Be mindful of noise, especially outdoors at night. We want zero issues with neighbors or local authorities.

Respect the Commitment
Payment timelines are firm. The commissioner is coordinating large upfront costs — not financing the trip.

🚫 House Rules (Non-Negotiable)
Quiet Hours & Noise
Keep outdoor noise under control late at night. Complaints put our deposit at risk.

No Unauthorized Guests
Only confirmed attendees are allowed on the property. No outside visitors or parties.

Parking & Ride Coordination
Follow all parking guidelines and use designated areas for vehicles and pickups.

Shared Space Responsibility
Clean up nightly. No personal items or mess left in common areas.

Trash Management
Take out trash regularly to keep the house clean and organized.

Damage Reporting
If anything breaks, report it immediately. Respect the property at all times.

Pool & Outdoor Rules
No glass near the pool or spa — use plastic cups or cans only.

Checkout Expectations
Leave the house in good condition — trash removed, dishes handled, and spaces reset.

💰 Trip Commitments & Financials
Non-Refundable Deposit
All deposits are non-refundable. This secures your spot and covers upfront costs already committed.

Payment Deadlines
All deadlines are firm. The commissioner is fronting significant expenses and cannot chase payments.

Spot Confirmation
Your spot is only confirmed once your deposit is received.

Cancellation Policy
If you back out, you are still responsible for your spot unless a replacement is found. If a replacement takes your place, reimbursement may be arranged.

Shared Expenses
Housing and group stock-up costs are split evenly among all attendees.
Transportation is handled individually, with estimated ride costs provided.

Golf Costs
Golf expenses apply only to those participating in the rounds.

Headcount Impact
Final cost per person adjusts based on attendance:

More golfers = higher per-person golf cost

More non-golfers/significant others = lower shared cost per person
